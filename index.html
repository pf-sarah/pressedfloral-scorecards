How to use this app

Follow these steps each month to set up goals, enter actuals, and build employee scorecards.

1
Upload Rippling Data

Start each month by uploading your Rippling CSV. This pre-loads employee names, roles, departments, locations, and pay rates so you don't have to enter them manually.

  • Go to Rippling Data in the sidebar
  • Select the payroll month
  • Upload the Active_Employees_with_Hourly_and_Annual_Base_Pay.csv file
  • Review the preview and click Save to App
2
Build Your Goal Bank

The Goal Bank is your permanent library of goals. Goals don't expire — they're reused month to month and pulled into scorecards. Set this up once and update as needed.

  • Go to Goal Bank in the sidebar
  • Click + Add Goal to Bank at the bottom
  • Set the type: Company (everyone), Department (dept-wide), or Individual (role-specific)
  • Fill in goal name, default target, minimum, and whether lower is better
  • Goals can be deactivated (hidden from scorecards) without being deleted
  • Repeat for all company, department, and individual goals
3
Enter Company & Department Actuals

At month end, enter the actual results for company and department goals. These are shared across all employees — enter them once and they'll be available when building scorecards.

  • Go to Enter Actuals in the sidebar
  • Select the month from the filter at the top
  • Type the actual result in the Actual column for each goal
  • Click the ✓ button on each row to save
  • Individual goal actuals are entered per-employee in the Build Scorecard step
4
Build Individual Scorecards

For each employee, build their scorecard by selecting goals from the bank, setting weights, entering individual actuals, and submitting. Company and department actuals you already entered will pre-fill automatically.

  • Go to Build Scorecard in the sidebar
  • Type the employee's name — Rippling data auto-fills their role, location, and pay info
  • Select the scorecard month
  • Goals from the bank load automatically based on their role and department
  • Set a weight for each goal (all weights must total 100%)
  • Override the target per goal if this month's target differs from the default
  • Enter actuals for individual goals — company and dept actuals are pre-filled
  • Use + Misc Goal to add a one-off goal unique to this employee
  • Use Add more goals from bank to include additional goals beyond the defaults
  • Review the calculated bonus summary at the bottom, then click Submit Scorecard
5
Review Historical Data

Search and review all submitted scorecards. Filter by period, employee, department, or location. Export to CSV for payroll or further analysis.

  • Go to Historical Data in the sidebar
  • Select a period and optionally filter by location or department
  • Click Search to view results
  • Each row shows goal-level detail — achievement, actuals, and bonus contribution
  • Goals that didn't meet their minimum show Below min with $0 bonus contribution
  • Click ↓ Export filtered results CSV to download a full spreadsheet
Tips
  • All goal weights on a scorecard must total exactly 100% — the builder warns you if they don't
  • Scorecards are capped at 200% total weighted achievement
  • Achievements of 120%+ are flagged for review but not capped
  • If a goal's actual doesn't meet the minimum threshold, that goal contributes $0 to the bonus
  • Company and department actuals only need to be entered once — they apply to all scorecards for that month
  • Goals can be deactivated in the Goal Bank without deleting them, keeping history intact
